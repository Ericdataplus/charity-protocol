‚ö†Ô∏è What Could Go Wrong

Complete transparency means telling you the risks too:

Smart Contract Bug

Code vulnerability could be exploited. We mitigate with audits and bug bounties.

Vendor Collusion

What if Amazon's payment address changes? Requires 67% vote + 7-day delay to update.

Charity Resells Products

A charity could sell donated goods for cash. Community can vote to remove bad actors.

Stablecoin Depeg

USDC could lose its $1 peg. We use the most regulated, audited stablecoin available.

Voting Manipulation

Coordinated groups could sway votes. Quadratic voting limits whale power.

Regulatory Shutdown

Governments could ban crypto. We comply with laws and maintain transparency.
